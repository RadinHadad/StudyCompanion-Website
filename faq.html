Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I add a new task?

You can add a new task by clicking the "Add Task" button on your dashboard, then filling in the due date, task name, and priority level.

Q2: Can I edit or remove tasks later?

Yes! Use the "Edit Task" or "Remove Task" buttons on the dashboard sidebar to update or delete existing tasks.

Q3: How does the progress tracking work?

Update your progress on any task via the "Update Progress" button. You can log your progress summary, confidence level, and percentage complete.

Q5: Can I customize priorities beyond 1 to 10?

The priority scale is from 1 to 10 for simplicity, but feel free to use any number within that range to suit your needs.

Q6: What devices support Study Companion?

Study Companion is designed to work on desktops, laptops, tablets, and mobile devices with modern browsers.

Q7: Will there be an app version?

We are exploring mobile app versions for both iOS and Android. Stay tuned for announcements!

Q8: How can I provide feedback or request features?

You can send feedback via the contact form on the homepage or email us directly at support@studycompanion.com.

Q9: Can I add videos or media to my tasks?

Currently, tasks support text only, but you can access the Media page to view helpful study videos curated for you.

Q10: Is Study Companion free to use?

Yes! Study Companion is completely free to use with no ads.
