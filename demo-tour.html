How to use this planner effectively

Organise Due Dates – Track Progress – Prioritise Tasks – Complete On Time

Study Companion is your most friendly and simplistic study buddy that will remove all the due dates for exams and homework from your head to a readable screen. Whether you're tracking a major assessment or a simple to-do list, this planner keeps you focused and stress-free.

Scheduling:

Create a clear plan for your week. Add due dates for homework, assignments, or study sessions. No more forgetting when tasks are due — everything is logged in one place.

Tasks:

Enter tasks with their importance (priority), due date, and details. Tasks are sorted for you, so you know exactly what to focus on next. Progress updates help you stay on track.

Not just an ordinary to-do list–

Study Companion was specially crafted for busy students. Unlike to-do lists that are too simple or schedules that are too complex, Study Companion brings together the best of both worlds.

Unique features such as task prioritisation remind you of what needs your attention first. You’ll also benefit from insightful progress logs, showing how much you've completed and how confident you feel.
